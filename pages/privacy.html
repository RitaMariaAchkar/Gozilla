Our Three Core Privacy Principles

Gozilla is dedicated to protecting your personal data. Below is our Data Privacy Policy, which outlines the principles and practices guiding how we handle your information at Gozilla.
We Protect Your Personal Data
We take strong, appropriate measures to prevent loss, misuse, or unauthorized disclosure of your personal data, including encryption and secure handling.
We Handle Your Data Responsibly
We only collect the information necessary to provide our services and give you full control over your personal data.
We Share Data Only When Necessary
Gozilla's teams and service providers access your personal data solely when required—for fulfilling orders, investigating fraud, or improving our products and services.

Shoppers:

  • Your full name as provided in the app, used to accurately locate your address for order delivery.
  • The name and address of the store you ordered from.
  • A unique, randomly generated order code to share with the store when picking up your order (the “Generated Order Code”).

Merchants:

  • Your first name and the initial of your last name.
  • The items included in the order.
  • The Generated Order Code.
  • A unique, randomly assigned user identifier used for order-related calls. These calls are routed through the Gozilla contact center, keeping your phone number private from the store.

Shoppers:

  • Your full name as provided in the app, used to accurately locate your address for delivery.
  • Your address and delivery details, ensuring a smooth delivery experience.
  • A button allowing the driver to contact you via the app (in-app call) or your phone number for order updates. In-app calls hide your phone number from the driver.
  • All customer-driver communication will occur only through in-app chat or call. In the upcoming app update, shoppers' phone numbers will remain completely private.

Merchants:

  • The same information visible at order placement will remain accessible while the order is in transit.
  • This includes the customer's first name and initial of their last name, the items ordered, the Generated Order Code, and a unique user identifier.

Shoppers:

  • All the above-mentioned personal information visible upon placing an order will no longer be available as soon as your order is delivered. This includes your full name provided on the app, your address and delivery details, and the ability to contact you.

Merchants:

  • The same information visible upon placing an order and when your order is on the way. This includes your first name and the first letter of your last name, the items you ordered, the Generated Order Code, and a random unique user identifier.
  • In addition, and in case you rate your order below 5 stars, the store you ordered from will be able to contact you within a 48 hour period via a random unique user identifier code to make things right! These calls are routed through the Gozilla contact center without the store having access to your phone number.

How does the information you provide influence your experience in the app?

  • Location:
    Sharing your location allows us to show nearby stores, estimate delivery times, and ensure your order reaches you as quickly as possible
  • Online Payments:
    When you pay online, we place a temporary authorization on your card for the order amount. After your order is completed, the final charge is applied. Your card information is securely handled by certified payment providers with top-level encryption (PCI compliance). Gozilla does not store or access your credit card details, and they remain inaccessible to both Gozilla employees and drivers.
